# Ankarsrum, Customer Journey, United Kingdom (Draft)

Single-market deep dive of the customer journey on Ankarsrum's UK market, delivered by Cyntora. The angle: the UK is an emerging market, still being built, in contrast to the mature Sweden and US.

**Market:** United Kingdom (`ankarsrum.com/uk/`)
**Period:** 15 May 2025 to 14 May 2026
**Version:** 1.1 (UK draft, third market after Sweden and USA; added executive summary + impact/effort/KPI per rec)
**Delivered:** May 2026

## Scope

This deck is a data-driven foundation for the UK market. It describes how the UK behaves in the channels and analytics that are measurable today (GA4, GSC, Meta Ads, Google Ads, WooCommerce). It is intended as an input to the strategy agency's continued customer journey, persona and positioning work, not as a replacement for that work.
